About

I am an Instructor and Faculty Researcher at Harvard Medical School, Department of Ophthalmology and AI scientist at Mass Eye and Ear, specializing in AI for Healthcare. My work focuses on advancing medical knowledge through research and educating the next generation of healthcare professionals.

My research interests include Surgical AI, Intervention Planning, and Early Diagnosis/Prognosis. Visit the projects page. I am committed to open-science and translating research findings into clinical practice to improve patient outcomes.

I actively teach and supervise students worldwide (RAs page). Feel free to reach out if you're interested in our research.
